Ages of Man
The goal of the "Ages of Man" project is to create computer generated art inspired by impactful social events. Raise awareness and transparency in every day happenings around the world. The creators experiment with various computer aided visualization techniques such as artificial intelligence, machine learning and data mining and advanced data visualization.

#tweetMeNot - @realDonaldTrump tweets

#TweetMeNot was created to symbolize the impact of social networks in politics. The dataset used for the experiment contains tweets from @realDonaldTrump. The base visualization is created with a popular data visualization technique called word cloud, where the occurrence frequency defines the scaled representation of each word extracted from the dataset. The data has been pre-cleansed, and certain stop words (have, is, does etc.) have been filtered to create a better result. The color alteration animation on top is generated with the colors of the US flag.
